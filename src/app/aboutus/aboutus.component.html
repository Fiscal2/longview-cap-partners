About Us

Founded in 2004 by Ira Goldberg and his father Edward Goldberg. Edward started in the mailrooms of Merrill Lynch and worked his way to be Executive Vice President - Ira followed in his footsteps and also worked for Merrill in Institutional Fixed Income and Trading. After Ed's 42 years of service and Ira's 14, they decided to start their own family office. Starting in 2015, Edward retired and Ira took on the role as CEO and Chairman.

Our Value

Longview Capital's personalized approach to our client relationships manages to logically address the challenges faced by clients along with the concerns any investors may have. A partnership with Longview Capital enables clients to gain access to relationships that can improve the chances of a successful capital raise. Moreover, it enables the client to generate attractive returns without further distractions from marketing initiatives.
